# HostelHub Video Explanation Script (3–5 Minutes)

Hello! I’m excited to show you HostelHub, my hostel issue-tracking app for Kalvium Concept-3.

First, I used Design Thinking. I talked to students and staff to understand their problems. I defined the main problem: students need a fast, clear way to report and track issues.

Next, I designed four screens in Figma: Login, Dashboard, Report Issue, and Issue List. Each screen is simple and student-friendly.

Then, I built the UI in Flutter. I mapped Figma elements to Flutter widgets like Text, Column, Card, and Button. I used MediaQuery and LayoutBuilder to make the app responsive.

For example, on a tablet, the issue list and details show side by side. On a phone, they stack vertically. I avoided fixed sizes so the app works everywhere.

In summary, I translated my Figma design into Flutter by matching the layout and colors, then making sure it works well on all devices. Design Thinking helped me focus on real user needs, and Flutter made it easy to build a responsive app.

Thank you!
